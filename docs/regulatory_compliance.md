# ⚖️ Feza-X: Regulatory & De-orbiting Compliance

Ensuring the mission adheres to international space laws and sustainability standards.

## 1. ITU Frequency Filing
- **Band:** Amateur/Experimental Satellite Service (437MHz / 2.4GHz).
- **Coordination:** Filed through the national agency (TUA/BTK) for IARU coordination.
- **Callsign:** Assigned upon flight model registration.

## 2. Space Debris Mitigation (ESA Zero Debris)
- **Natural Decay:** 500km altitude ensures re-entry within <12 years (well below the 25-year limit).
- **Passivation:** Batteries and EPS are passivated (discharged) at the end of life to prevent explosion.
- **Trackability:** Integrated passive laser retroreflectors for precise orbit determination.

## 3. Licensing & Insurance
- **Space Liability:** Compliant with the Outer Space Treaty (1967).
- **Launching State:** Republic of Türkiye.
